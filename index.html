I started Android development when I finished my first year of Computer Engineering department. In that summer, I was trying to find something new that affect my career and help to improve myself. I have already took the Java programming language course and Object Oriented Programming course so I decided to develop something by usign Java.

I started to investigate from internet and I found myself one of the good courses of Android mobile development. I immediately bought the book of the source and started to learning Android. I have no experience before and I didn't know any person that I consulted to him/her. I was alone and I was new in development also learning new things. I started to learning Android Application development and in 1 year process I developed 2 Android projects. It was not enough and everyday new technologies born so I had to improve myself everyday. Then I started my internship and I learned a lot of new things then I reliazed that even 1 year experience I did not know so many things. In this internship, I started to new course that published by Udacity. This courses was the best one for me. Then I started to university again. My first project was about daily news and I was 3 months project.
